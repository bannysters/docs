### `AuthTimeLeftUnix`

> **Type:** `number` (Unix timestamp)\
> **Example:** `1700000000`

Time remaining on the user's key in seconds. Useful for calculating time-based restrictions.

**Convert to seconds left:**

```lua

print("Time left: " .. secondsLeft .. " seconds")
```
