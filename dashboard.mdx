The 9Auth web dashboard is your main interface for managing scripts, keys, and server integrations.

## Features

- **Sign in with Discord**: Secure OAuth-based authentication.
- **Add Bot to Server**: Invite the 9Auth bot to manage access within your community.
- **Upload Scripts**: Add and manage your scripts through a simple UI.
- **Manage Keys**: Create, revoke, or distribute keys to users.
- **Manage Users**: Track who has access, usage stats, and HWID resets.
- **Webhooks**: Connect events (key usage, redemption, etc.) to your own systems.
